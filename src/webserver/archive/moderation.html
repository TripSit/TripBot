Moderation Info

We have community moderation features!

Three on a message activates a timeout!
- One-hour timeout. Mods will review context and take further action asappropriate.
- One-day timeout & KICK request. Kicked after a day if modsdon't say otherwise.
- One-day timeout & BAN request. Kicked after a day if modsdon't say otherwise.
- Request age verification. Quarantines the user from 18+ content pending review.
